# Privacy Policy

Token Cost Widget does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

## What this app does

- Reads coding agent usage logs stored locally on your device to calculate token usage and cost statistics.
- All parsing and cost aggregation is performed locally by the bundled ccusage command-line tool.
- To keep displayed costs accurate for newly released models, the app fetches public model pricing data over the network. This request does not include your usage logs or any personal information.

## What this app does not do

- Does not collect, store, or transmit your usage logs, code, or any personal information.
- Does not use analytics, telemetry, or advertising.
- Does not require sign-in or an account.
